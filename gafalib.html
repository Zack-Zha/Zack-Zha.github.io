Brief

GAFAlib is a platform that connects students to their campus affairs, helping them to process affairs and services.

GAFAlib is a platform that connects students to their campus affairs, helping them to process their affairs and apply for school services. It aims to enable students to quickly process their affairs and manage their academic careers through an easy-to-use and highly integrated functional system.

Solutions

Homepage Features

  • Quick Access Links: Direct buttons to frequently used services, informed by user behavior and preferences identified in research.
  • Notifications: Alerts for upcoming deadlines, events, and announcements, ensuring users stay informed without feeling overwhelmed.
  • Personalized Dashboard: A customizable space where students can pin their most-used services, addressing the need for individualization.

Service Hall Features

  • Centralized Services: A comprehensive list of campus services including counseling, academic support, and extracurricular activities, organized to prevent information overload.
  • Booking System: An integrated system for scheduling appointments and reservations that is easy to navigate for users with varying tech skills.
  • FAQs and Resources: Clearly categorized access to common questions and related resources to assist students in finding answers quickly without frustration.

Transaction Hall Features

  • Transaction Management: A platform that allows users to submit requests, applications, and documents effortlessly while providing clear guidance at each step.
  • Status Tracking: Real-time updates on the status of submitted requests, designed to enhance transparency and user confidence.
  • Feedback System: An option for users to provide feedback on services and transactions to identify areas for improvement, fostering an environment of continuous enhancement.

Next Steps

To ensure the successful implementation of the Smart Portal System, the following steps will be undertaken:

  • Supplement the design of the teacher's side and invite teachers to experience it and do usability testing.
  • Collaborate with the development team to build the portal based on the final designs, ensuring all features are user-friendly and inclusive.
  • Monitor user engagement and gather feedback for ongoing improvements and feature enhancements, ensuring the platform evolves alongside user needs.
